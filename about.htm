About

Time to meet Mama Bear. Mama Bears real name is Nadia Tia Marie Morse. Nadia or Mama Bear is a licensed by the state of California to run her in home daycare. At Mama Bear's Daycare safety is first and foremost that is why we make sure to comply with all state standards for safety. We make sure to do our state inspections so that we can give your children the safe and enjoyable environment they need. At Mama Bear's daycare we are focused on activities that stimulate both your childs mind and body. We make sure that the children get at least one hour of play time a day and that they eat nutritional foods to stimulate there bodies. We keep there brain active with many learning activities that teach teamwork as well as problem solving skills. We strive to help your children build the social skills that they will need when they start school. Mama Bear is a mother to three kids so she knows how to make them feel safe and comfortable in this new environment. So schedule a tour today. We can't waite to see you!
